1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following types of information.

Information you provide us directly:

  • Your full name, e-mail address and password when you register for a doctorsinfo.pk account.
  • Profile information that you provide for your user profile (e.g., gender, phone number, year of birth).
  • Your current city from your IP address.

2. WHY DO WE COLLECT THAT INFORMATION OF YOURS

  • Full name (To help other patients have a sense of security, when you left a feedback about a doctor or ask a question on discussion forum.)
  • email-address (To help you recover your account, in-case you have lost access to your account and approve your activity at doctorsinfo.pk )
  • Gender (To personalize the content on our site according to your gender.)
  • Phone number (To inform you about any changes in practice information for your favorite Practitioners.)
  • Year of Birth (To personalize the content on our site according to your age group.)
  • City (To show you doctors relevant to your city.)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • help you efficiently access your personal information after you sign in
  • remember information so you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit the Service.
  • provide personalized content and information to you and others, which could include on-line ads.
  • provide, improve, test, and monitor the effectiveness of our Service
  • monitor metrics such as total number of visitors, traffic, and demographic patterns
  • diagnose or fix technical faults.

4. SHARING OF YOUR INFORMATION

  • We will not rent or sell your information to third parties outside doctorsinfo.pk without your consent, except as noted in this Policy.
  • If we sell or otherwise transfer part or the whole of doctorsinfo.pk or our assets to another organization (e.g., in the course of a transaction like a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, dissolution, liquidation), your information such as full name and email address, User Content and any other information collected through the Service may be among the items sold or transferred. You will continue to own your User Content. The buyer or transferee will have to honor the commitments we have made in this Privacy Policy.

5. YOUR CHOICES ABOUT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

  • Update your account at any time by logging in and changing your profile settings.
  • Un-subscribe from emails from us by clicking on the "un-subscribe link" provided in such emails.
  • Following termination or deactivation of your account, doctorsinfo.pk may retain information (including your profile information) and User Content for a reasonable time for backup, archival, and/or audit purposes.
